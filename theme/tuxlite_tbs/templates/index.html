About

Scientific Software Days is an ongoing meeting of users and producers of scientific software, with presentations by scientific software tool makers and the users of their tools. The objective is to build cross-disciplinary community and skills in the diverse set of users and developers of scientific software, both academic and industrial.

Sponsors

Scientific Software Days is proudly sponsored by the Jackson School of Geosciences, Texas Advanced Computing Center, and the Insitute for Computational Engineering and Sciences.
